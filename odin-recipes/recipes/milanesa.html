MILANESA

Description

This is the best dish you will ever eat. Generally it is a peace of breaded roast-beef. Best accompanied with fries

Ingredients

  • 1Kg of Roast-Beef
  • 200g Bread Crumb
  • 2 Eggs
  • Some Parsley
  • 2-3 Garlic teeths
  • Some Mustard
  • Salt to taste
  • Fries

Steps

  1. Mix 2 eggs with chopped garlic and parsley, salt and mustard
  2. Remove fat from roast beef and then slice it thinly
  3. Inmerse meat peaces in the mixed egg
  4. Inmerse meat peaces in bread crumb
  5. Fry them, in a deep oil-bath
  6. Prepare fries
